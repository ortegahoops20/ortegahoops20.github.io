About Us

We are a group of high school students who love the game of basketball, and look to help kids in our community improve their game.

Skill Development

Our coaches have several years of experience in competitive basketball, including at the high school level. We aim to develop our players' skills, such as catch-and-shoot jumpers and defensive slides, in order to give them the confidence to use these tools in real games.

Safety

All kids will stay masked, and we will do our part to enforce the county safety rules. All parents will also be informed of the fact that basketball is a contact sport and that the kids will be coming close to each other.

Fun!

We understand that kids have limited opportunities to enjoy outdoor recreational activites at this given time, so we hope to provide a safe space where kids can have fun and stay active!
